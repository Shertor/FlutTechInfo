Features

Different plate types and boundary conditions

Problem Statement

Natural plate vibrations

The plate free vibrations problem is governed by the Germain-Lagrange plate equation. One should apply certain descritisation according to boundary conditions and solve with the Bubnov — Galerkin method

See more
Plate Flutter

For the plate flutter problem, a mathematical model of a plate flutter, constructed by A.A. Ilyushin and I.A. Kiiko, is adopted, which allows taking into account an arbitrary direction of the incoming flow vector.
